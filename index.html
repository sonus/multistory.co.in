SURAJ VARMA

DIRECTOR|PRODUCER|TEACHER|

MENTOR

 Suraj Varma started his career assisting, the late Ismail Merchant and James Ivory of Merchant Ivory Productions (MIP, London and New York) in their films, “Cotton Mary” and ‘Mystic Masseur’.
 
After working in foreign lands, Suraj returned to India to work with Rajiv Menon in his production house, Rajiv Menon Productions and worked with Rajiv on his TV commercials. 

Suraj worked as a Creative Director for Accel Animation Studios, Trivandrum. He directed 78 episodes of the 3D animated series – “Raju the rickshaw” targeted at pre-school kids. The series is distributed by French Company, CyberSchool Studios and is now also available at Amazon Prime.
